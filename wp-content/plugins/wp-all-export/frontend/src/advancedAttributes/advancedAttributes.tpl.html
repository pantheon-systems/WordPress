Unit Prices

These attributes allow you to submit pricing for products that rely on unit pricing. The 'unit pricing measure' attribute defines the measure and dimension of an item (e.g. 150g). The 'unit pricing base measure attribute specifies your preference of the denominator of the unit price (e.g. 100g).

For example, if the 'price' is 3 USD, 'unit pricing measure' is 150g and 'unit pricing base measure' is 100g, the unit price would be '2 USD/200g'.

Additional Attributes

Expiration Date

This is the date that an item listing will expire. If you do not provide this attribute, items will expire and no longer appear in Google Shopping results after 30 days. You cannot use thi attribute to extend the expiration period to longer than 30 days.

Energy Efficiency Class

This attribute allows you to submit the energy label for your applicable products in feeds targeting European Union countries and switzerland.
